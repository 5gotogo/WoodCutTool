What it does

Address Label Maker & Envelopes is the easiest way to create beautiful mailing labels, shipping labels, and personalized envelopes. Perfect for home, office, small businesses, and holiday gifts.

Whether you’re sending packages, printing return address labels, or designing decorative stickers, this app helps you create professional results in minutes.

  • KEY FEATURES
  • Create custom address labels and envelopes
  • Supports Avery label templates (5160, 5161, 5163, and more)
  • Add holiday and decorative stickers
  • Import addresses directly from Contacts
  • Adjust text size, layout, spacing, and alignment
  • Live preview with drag-to-reposition
  • Print your designs or export to PDF
  • MAILING & SHIPPING MADE EASY

Design labels for packages, letters, invitations, and gift tags. Perfect for USPS, UPS, FedEx, and international shipping.

  • CUSTOMIZE YOUR ENVELOPES

Add sender and recipient information, choose envelope size (#10, A6, A7), and preview before printing.

  • STICKERS & DECORATIONS

Personalize your labels with beautiful stickers—ideal for Christmas, holidays, birthdays, and crafts.

  • FAST WORKFLOW

Just enter your address, choose a template, adjust the layout, and print. No complicated setup required.

  • PERFECT FOR
  • Holiday cards & Christmas mail
  • Small business shipping
  • Wedding invitations
  • Gift labels & return address labels
  • Home office organization
  • Teachers, shops, Etsy sellers

Start creating beautiful, printable address labels today!
