What it does

Atomic Clock – Precision Time is a professional precision clock and NTP time synchronization utility for anyone who needs highly accurate time directly on their device.

Use reliable network time synchronization to view precise time with millisecond-level detail. Compare your device clock with network time, check sync offset and latency, switch between local time and UTC, and keep the screen on when you need a stable reference clock.

Key features:

  • NTP-based time synchronization
  • Millisecond or 1/60-second detail
  • Local time and UTC display
  • Device offset and network latency indicators
  • Automatic sync with configurable intervals
  • Six clock styles: circular dots, analog, minimalist, binary, neon glow, and flip clock
  • Custom accent colors and multi-color display
  • Night mode and keep-screen-on option
  • Privacy-focused: no account, no analytics, no tracking

Atomic Clock – Precision Time is built for precise timing, clock drift checks, latency awareness, and a clean daily precision clock.
