What it does

BlockFit: Block Puzzle is a relaxing yet addictive block puzzle game designed to keep your mind engaged and entertained.

Drag and drop colorful blocks into the board, fill rows or columns to clear them, and score as high as you can. Plan your moves carefully, because once there’s no room left, the game is over!

Why you’ll love BlockFit:

  • Simple and intuitive controls – just drag and drop
  • Endless gameplay with no time limit – perfect for relaxing
  • Satisfying animations and sound effects for every move
  • Track your best score and challenge yourself to beat it
  • Play offline anytime, no Wi-Fi needed

Whether you want a quick break or a long brain-training session, BlockFit is your go-to puzzle game. Download now and start fitting the blocks!
