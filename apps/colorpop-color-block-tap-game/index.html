What it does

Relax, tap, and pop!

ColorPop is a colorful and relaxing puzzle game made for all ages. Tap matching color blocks to clear them and score big. The more you pop at once, the higher your combo!

Features:

  • Clean and simple design with soothing colors
  • Easy to play, hard to master
  • Smart combo system for endless fun
  • No time limit, play at your own pace
  • Offline play supported

How to Play:

  • Tap groups of two or more same-colored blocks to pop them.
  • Clear larger groups for higher scores.
  • Reach the target to advance to the next level.

Download ColorPop today and start your relaxing color-matching journey!
