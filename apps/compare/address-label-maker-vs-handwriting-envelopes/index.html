Address Label Maker vs Handwriting Envelopes

Handwriting a few envelopes is fine. Handwriting fifty holiday cards is not. Address Label Maker turns your contacts into neat, printable address labels and envelopes, fast and consistent.

Where Address Label Maker & Envelope wins

For any volume of mail, printed labels are faster, neater, and reusable. The app pulls from Contacts and supports common Avery label sheets, so a stack of envelopes takes minutes.

When Handwriting Envelopes still makes sense

Handwriting adds a personal touch for a single card or a heartfelt note. For bulk mail, gift tags, or business envelopes, printed labels win on speed.

FAQ

Is an address label app faster than handwriting?

For more than a few envelopes, yes. It batch-prints neat labels from your contacts.

Does it support Avery label sheets?

Yes. It supports common Avery templates so labels line up on standard sheets.

Can it import addresses from Contacts?

Yes. You can pull addresses from Contacts instead of typing each one.

Is handwriting ever better?

For a single personal card, handwriting adds a personal touch. For bulk mail, printed labels are faster.
