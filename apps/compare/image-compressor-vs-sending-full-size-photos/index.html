Image Compressor vs Sending Full-Size Photos

Full-size photos bounce off email limits and clog inboxes. Image Compressor shrinks images to a sensible size and can bundle them into a ZIP so they send fast and arrive intact.

Where Image Compressor & ZIP wins

Compressing first means images actually send, fit under limits, and download quickly for the recipient, where full-size photos stall or bounce.

When Sending Full-Size Photos still makes sense

When you need full resolution for printing or editing, send the originals. For everyday sharing, compressed files are far more practical.

FAQ

Why compress images before sending?

Smaller files send faster, fit under email limits, and download quickly, unlike full-size photos.

Can it bundle photos into a ZIP?

Yes. Multiple images can be zipped into one file.

Can I control the quality?

Yes. Compression is adjustable.

When should I send full-size?

When you need full resolution for printing or editing.
