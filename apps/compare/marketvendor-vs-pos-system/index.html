MarketVendor vs a Full POS System

Where MarketVendor wins

MarketVendor is sized for a small seller: track sales and profit, see the day's totals, and keep records, without POS hardware, fees, or setup.

When a Full POS System still makes sense

A full POS wins for a shop with card processing, inventory integration, and staff. For a market stall, that is more than you need.

FAQ

Do I need a full POS for a market stall?

Usually not. MarketVendor tracks sales and profit without POS hardware, fees, or setup.

Does it require hardware?

No. It runs on your phone with no terminal or card reader.

Are there monthly fees?

No per-transaction or monthly POS fees; it is a simple app.

When is a POS better?

For a shop needing card processing, inventory integration, and multiple staff.
