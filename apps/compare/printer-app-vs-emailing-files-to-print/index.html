Printer App vs Emailing Files to Yourself to Print

Emailing a file to yourself, opening it on a computer, and printing from there is a lot of steps. Printer App prints PDFs and documents straight from your phone, skipping the round trip.

Where Printer App wins

Printing directly from the phone removes the email-to-computer detour, so a document goes from your hand to the printer in a couple of taps.

When Emailing Files to Print still makes sense

If you are already at your computer with the file open, printing there is fine. For phone-first printing, the app is much quicker.
