App Store description

What it does

CutList helps woodworkers, cabinet makers, and DIY builders turn their cut lists into optimized sheet layouts in seconds, right on your iPhone, fully offline.

Whether you’re building cabinets, shelves, drawers, or any panel project, CutList figures out the most efficient way to cut your parts from plywood, MDF, melamine, or any sheet stock. No more wasted material, no more guesswork.

============================

WHY WOODWORKERS LOVE CUTLIST

  • FAST LAYOUT GENERATION

Enter your sheet sizes and parts, tap Generate, and see optimized layouts instantly.

  • ON-DEVICE AI SCAN (PRO)

Snap a photo of handwritten or printed cut lists. AI OCR reads and imports parts automatically, fully offline.

  • STEP-BY-STEP CUT SEQUENCE

Follow clear cutting instructions with exact coordinates directly from your iPhone.

  • MATERIAL COST & WASTE TRACKING

Track material usage, waste percentage, and project costs before cutting.

  • INCH & METRIC SUPPORT
  • GRAIN DIRECTION LOCK

Keep wood grain patterns aligned correctly for cabinet doors and visible surfaces.

  • KERF & EDGE BANDING

Customize saw blade kerf and edge banding allowance for accurate layouts.

  • EXPORT TO PDF (PRO)

Export layouts and cut sequences as PDF files for printing or sharing.

  • AIRPRINT SUPPORT (PRO)

Print layouts directly from your iPhone using AirPrint.

  • PROJECT HISTORY

Save, reopen, edit, and duplicate projects anytime.

============================

SUBSCRIPTION REQUIRED FOR PRO FEATURES

CutList includes free basic layout generation features. Advanced features including AI Scan, PDF Export, AirPrint, unlimited projects, and additional professional tools require an active Pro subscription.

Subscription pricing is displayed inside the app and may vary by region.

Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions anytime in your Apple ID settings.

============================

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

CutList runs entirely on your device. No account required. No cloud uploads. No tracking. Your project data never leaves your iPhone.

============================

WHO IT’S FOR

  • Professional cabinet makers and carpenters
  • Furniture builders and woodworking shops
  • DIY woodworkers and home renovators
  • CNC operators planning sheet layouts
  • Anyone working with plywood, MDF, or panel materials
