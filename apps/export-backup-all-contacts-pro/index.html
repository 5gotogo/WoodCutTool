App Store description

What it does

Export Backup All Contacts Pro gives you full control of your address book. Export every contact, selected contacts, or cleaned contact lists to Excel (.xlsx), CSV, vCard (.vcf), or JSON, then share the file through Files, AirDrop, Mail, Messages, or any cloud app supported by iOS.

Whether you are switching phones, preparing a spreadsheet for work, building a customer list, cleaning a messy phonebook, or making a private backup before a device reset, the app keeps contact management fast and clear. Everything is designed for people who want a dependable contact exporter and backup tool without complicated setup.

============================

KEY FEATURES

  • EXPORT TO EXCEL, CSV, VCARD, AND JSON

Create files that work with spreadsheets, CRM tools, email services, address book apps, backup workflows, and developer tools.

  • CUSTOM FIELD SELECTION

Choose exactly what to include: names, company, job title, phones, emails, addresses, birthday, notes, URLs, messages, social profiles, tags, groups, and photos.

  • EXPORT PHOTOS WHEN NEEDED

Include contact images for complete backups, or turn photos off when you need a smaller file for sharing, spreadsheet work, or quick transfer.

  • FIND AND MERGE DUPLICATES

Scan for similar contacts, review duplicate groups, and merge entries while preserving unique phones, emails, addresses, notes, birthdays, tags, and other details.

  • SMART CLEANUP

Detect incomplete contacts, invalid phone numbers, and invalid email addresses. Clean data before export so your spreadsheet, backup, or imported address book is easier to read and maintain.

  • SECURE BACKUP AND RESTORE

Create local contact backups and restore them when needed. Optional password protection uses strong on-device encryption.

  • IMPORT CONTACT FILES

Bring contacts back from CSV, JSON, or spreadsheet-style files. Import and restore tools help you rebuild an address book after cleanup, migration, or device replacement.

  • TAGS, GROUPS, AND BATCH ACTIONS

Add tags, set groups, filter contacts, and handle batches without editing one card at a time.

  • EASY SHARING

Save to Files, send by email, move with AirDrop, attach to messages, or share to cloud storage apps already installed on your device.

  • DESIGNED FOR PRIVACY

Contact processing happens on your device. The app does not require an account, does not upload your address book to a server, and does not track your contacts. You stay in control of the files you create and share.

============================

USE IT FOR EVERYDAY CONTACT WORK

Build a spreadsheet of your full phonebook, transfer contacts to another account, keep a private JSON archive, prepare a vCard import file, clean invalid entries before sharing, or save an encrypted backup before changing phones. Large lists are easier to review because you can search, select, filter, and export only what you need.

============================

PRO SUBSCRIPTION

Export Backup All Contacts Pro includes professional tools for export, backup, restore, cleanup, duplicate management, import, and advanced contact workflows. Some features require an active Pro subscription.

Subscription pricing is shown inside the app and may vary by region. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID settings.

============================

PRIVACY FIRST

Your contacts are personal. Export Backup All Contacts Pro is built around local processing, clear sharing, and user control. Files are created only when you ask for them, and you decide where each export or backup goes.
