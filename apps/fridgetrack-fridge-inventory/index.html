What it does

FridgeTrack is a powerful fridge inventory and food expiration app that helps you organize what is inside your fridge, freezer, and pantry. With a clean and simple interface, FridgeTrack makes it easier to manage groceries, stay organized, and reduce food waste.

Whether you live alone, cook for a family, or prepare meals for the week, FridgeTrack gives you a smarter way to keep track of your kitchen inventory and always know what is still fresh.

Free Features

  • View items in your fridge, freezer, and pantry
  • Organize items by category such as meat, dairy, produce, and beverages
  • Search and filter your inventory instantly
  • Track purchase dates, opened dates, and frozen dates
  • Works offline with no account required

Note: Free users can browse and view inventory. Saving food items requires a Pro subscription.

FridgeTrack Pro Features

  • Save food items to your fridge, freezer, and pantry
  • Monitor expiration dates and get reminders before food goes bad
  • Use barcode scanning to quickly add groceries
  • Scan shopping receipts to automatically import purchased items
  • Use AI food recognition to add items by taking a photo
  • Smart reminders to help prevent forgotten food
  • Add and manage unlimited food items

Smart Kitchen Organization

FridgeTrack helps you manage groceries more efficiently and avoid unnecessary food waste. Instead of guessing what is in your fridge, you can keep everything organized in one place and stay on top of what needs to be used first.

Perfect For

  • Families managing weekly groceries
  • Meal preppers organizing food storage
  • Busy households reducing food waste
  • Students tracking limited groceries
  • Anyone who wants a smarter fridge inventory

Privacy First

FridgeTrack is designed with privacy in mind. Your food inventory stays on your device and no account is required.
