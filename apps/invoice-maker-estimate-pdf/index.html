App Store description

What it does

Invoice Maker: Estimate PDF turns your iPhone into a fast, private billing tool for freelancers, contractors, consultants, service providers, and small business owners. Create clean invoices and estimates in minutes, track who has paid, and send polished PDF documents to your clients — all without an account and without uploading anything to the cloud.

Create invoices in minutes

Pick a client, add line items with quantities and prices, set tax rates, issue dates, and due dates, and a professional invoice is ready before your coffee gets cold. Totals are calculated automatically, so there is no manual math and no formatting work. Make estimates and quotes the same way, then convert the job into an invoice when the work is confirmed.

Look professional on every document

Add your logo and signature, choose a clean template, and set your business name, address, tax number, and payment terms once. Every invoice and estimate comes out looking like it belongs to a real business, helping you get taken seriously and paid faster.

Track payments at a glance

See paid, unpaid, sent, and overdue invoices in one clear list, so you always know who still owes you. Mark invoices as paid, keep your billing history organized, and stop letting late payers slip through the cracks. A simple dashboard shows your totals so you can understand your income without a spreadsheet.

Send clean PDF documents

Export any invoice or estimate as a tidy PDF, then print it or share it instantly through the standard iOS share sheet to Mail, Messages, WhatsApp, and other apps. Your documents are client-ready and easy to send the moment they are done.

Manage clients and items

Keep your customer details organized so you never retype the same address twice. Save clients, reuse items, set default notes and terms, and customize your invoice and estimate numbering to match how you already work.

Built for privacy

Invoice Maker is designed around five clear privacy promises:

Offline: creating invoices and estimates, editing clients, generating PDFs, and saving all work without an internet connection.

No Login: open the app and start billing — no sign-up, no account, and no password just to invoice your own clients.

Privacy First: your business and client data is sensitive, and the app treats it that way from start to finish.

On Device: every invoice, estimate, client record, and PDF is created and stored on your iPhone.

No Cloud Upload: your invoices and client details are never uploaded to our servers — nothing leaves your device unless you choose to share it yourself.

Great for real work

Use Invoice Maker to bill clients on the go, hand an estimate to a customer before a job starts, send a receipt right after getting paid, or keep tidy records for tax time. Freelancers, designers, photographers, tradespeople, repair technicians, cleaners, tutors, consultants, and small business owners can create, send, and track invoices in just a few taps — even with no signal.

Simple workflow: add a client, build the invoice or estimate, export the PDF, and track payment. Some features and unlimited use may require an active subscription, which may include a free trial. Pricing and terms are shown clearly before any purchase, and you can cancel anytime.
