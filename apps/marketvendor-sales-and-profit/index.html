App Store description

What it does

MarketVendor turns your iPhone into a simple, private daily ledger for street vendors, market stalls, food trucks, and small pop-up businesses. Record what you sold, how much came in as cash or card, what it cost you, and how much profit you actually made — all in a few taps, right after you close up for the day.

Quick daily sales entry

Log each day's total sales split between cash and card, add a short note about how the day went, and move on. No spreadsheets, no complicated forms — just the numbers that matter, entered in seconds while you pack up your stall.

Know your real profit, not just revenue

Enter your ingredient costs, fuel costs, booth or stall fees, and any other expenses, and MarketVendor instantly calculates your gross profit and profit margin. See at a glance whether a busy day was actually a profitable one.

Compare locations and find your best spots

If you sell at more than one market, street corner, or event, MarketVendor tracks sales per location so you can see which spots bring in the most revenue and which ones are not worth the trip.

See your top-selling dishes and items

Track quantities sold for each menu item and instantly see which dishes or products are your bestsellers each week, helping you plan prep, stock, and pricing.

Spot trends over time

Built-in charts show your revenue trend, daily profit, and sales by day of the week, so you can spot your busiest days, slow seasons, and whether costs are creeping up.

Built for any currency

Choose the currency that matches where you do business, and every total, chart, and report updates to match.

Export for your accountant or tax filing

Export your sales history as a CSV file for spreadsheets, or as a clean PDF report ready for bookkeeping and tax filing — covering any date range you choose, from this month to your full history.

Built for privacy

MarketVendor is designed around five clear promises:

Offline: entering sales, tracking costs, viewing charts, and managing your menu and locations all work without an internet connection.

No Login: open the app and start logging your sales — no sign-up, no account, and no password required.

Privacy First: your sales numbers, costs, and profit margins are your business, and the app treats them that way from start to finish.

On Device: all of your records, calculations, and reports are generated on your iPhone or iPad.

No Cloud Upload: your data is never uploaded to our servers — nothing leaves your device unless you choose to export and share it yourself.

Built for real working days

Whether you run a food truck, a night market stall, a farmers market booth, a flea market table, or a small pop-up shop, MarketVendor fits into the rhythm of your day. Quickly log sales in the morning rush, check yesterday's numbers during a slow afternoon, or review the week's profit before deciding where to set up next.

How it works

1. At the end of the day, enter your total cash and card sales and any costs.

2. Add what you sold from your menu, and pick your location if you sell at more than one spot.

3. Check your dashboard for today's profit, weekly trends, and your best-selling items.

4. Export a CSV or PDF report whenever you need it for bookkeeping or taxes.
