What it does

PickOne is a smart decision assistant designed to help you reflect, track, and improve your choices over time — not just spin a wheel.

Whether you’re deciding what to eat, where to go, what to buy, or how to plan your day, PickOne combines randomness, weighted logic, and daily reflection prompts to help you make more intentional decisions.

Key Features

1、Spin the Wheel

Create your own option lists and spin the wheel to get an instant result. Perfect for groups, daily routines, and quick choices.

2、Weighted Decision Mode

Assign custom weights to important options. Prioritize what matters most and let probability guide smarter outcomes.

3、Daily Fortune Prompts

Ask a question and receive a reflection-based prompt designed to encourage mindful decision-making — not predictions.

4、Decision History & Insights

Track every decision you make. Discover patterns, trends, and personal habits over time with built-in insights.

5、Privacy-First Design

No account. No login. No data collection. All decisions stay on your device.

6、Designed for Everyday Life

Use PickOne for:

  • What to eat today
  • What to buy
  • Where to travel
  • Daily habits and routines
  • Team and family decisions
  • Study and productivity planning

PickOne is more than a random choice generator — it’s a personal decision system that grows with you.
