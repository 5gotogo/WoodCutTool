App Store description

What it does

Printer App turns your iPhone and iPad into a simple, private mobile print station. Print PDFs, documents, photos, and web pages to compatible Wi-Fi, wireless, AirPrint, and network printers at home, the office, school, or your local print shop. No cables, no computer, no hassle.

Print almost anything

Send PDFs, Word and text documents, JPEG and PNG photos, scanned pages, and full web pages straight to your printer. Import from the Files app and iCloud Drive, pick photos from your library, scan paper with the camera, or open files shared from Mail and other apps. Whatever you need on paper, Printer App helps you get it there fast.

Scan to PDF with your camera

Use your camera to turn paper documents, receipts, forms, and notes into clean, sharp PDFs that are ready to print or keep. Edge detection helps capture neat pages, so a quick photo becomes a tidy, printable document in seconds.

Advanced print settings, in your control

Go beyond a basic print button. Choose a page range, set the number of copies, pick paper size (A4, Letter, Legal, A3, A5), switch between portrait and landscape, scale to fit or fill the page, print in color or black and white, and turn on double-sided printing. Get the result you want before the paper comes out.

Multi-image layouts for photos

Print more than one image per page. Place 1, 2, 4, 6, or 9 photos on a single sheet with adjustable spacing, margins, and fit mode, and add optional captions. Perfect for photo sheets, contact prints, handouts, and saving paper.

Preview every page before you print

See a thumbnail of every page before printing. Rotate pages, delete the ones you do not need, reorder them, and choose exactly which pages to print. A clear page count and file size keep you informed, so you never waste paper on a layout you did not want.

Clean web page printing

Load any web page and print it without the clutter. Print the page directly or save it as a PDF first so you can keep a copy, share it, or print it later.

Built for privacy

Printer App is designed around five clear promises:

Offline: importing, scanning, page editing, layout, preview, and saving all work without an internet connection. AirPrint uses your local network to reach your printer.

No Login: open the app and start printing — no sign-up, no account, and no password just to print your own files.

Privacy First: your documents and photos are personal, and the app treats them that way from start to finish.

On Device: file handling, scanning, PDF creation, image layout, and previews all happen on your iPhone or iPad.

No Cloud Upload: your files are never uploaded to our servers — nothing leaves your device unless you choose to share or print it yourself.

Great for everyday life and real work

Print boarding passes, tickets, and confirmations before a trip. Print homework, assignments, and study notes for school. Print invoices, contracts, shipping labels, and forms for work. Print recipes, coloring pages, and photos at home. Students, freelancers, small business owners, real estate and trades workers, teachers, and busy families can all print in just a few taps.

How it works

1. Add a file, photo, scan, or web page.

2. Preview the pages and adjust settings if you need to.

3. Tap Print and choose your AirPrint printer.

AirPrint printers on the same Wi-Fi network are detected automatically by iOS, so there is usually nothing to set up. Paper size, copies, and printer options also appear in the system print panel at print time.

Some features and unlimited use may require an active subscription, which may include a free trial. Pricing and terms are shown clearly before any purchase, and you can cancel anytime.
