What it does

Priviscribe is a private offline speech to text app built for meetings, interviews, lectures, voice notes, and everyday audio transcription. It helps you turn spoken words into clear text on your device, so you can capture ideas, organize conversations, and review important details without relying on cloud processing.

Designed for privacy-focused users, Priviscribe keeps your recordings and transcripts on your device. There is no account required, no server upload, and no need to send sensitive audio to external platforms. Whether you are documenting business meetings, recording class discussions, transcribing interviews, or saving personal voice notes, Priviscribe gives you a secure and simple way to convert speech into text.

Key Features

  • Offline Speech to Text

Transcribe audio directly on your device with no internet required for core transcription workflows. Your voice recordings and transcripts stay private and under your control.

  • Real-Time Transcription

See text appear while recording so you can follow conversations, review spoken content faster, and capture meeting notes as they happen.

  • Speaker Labeling for Meetings and Interviews

Priviscribe can separate different speakers in conversations, making it easier to understand who said what during meetings, interviews, discussions, and group sessions.

  • Rename and Organize Speakers

Edit speaker names for cleaner transcripts and more readable meeting notes. This is especially useful for team meetings, research calls, class discussions, and interview recordings.

  • Private Meeting Notes

Capture meeting discussions and convert them into searchable text without sending confidential company conversations to the cloud.

  • Lecture and Study Transcripts

Record lessons, lectures, seminars, and study sessions, then review transcripts later for note-taking, summaries, and revision.

  • Interview Transcription

Useful for journalists, researchers, students, creators, and professionals who need fast voice transcription with speaker separation.

  • Voice Notes and Idea Capture

Quickly record ideas, reminders, brainstorming sessions, and personal thoughts, then turn them into text you can save, copy, or share.

  • Export and Share

Copy transcripts, save text, and share notes to your favorite apps, email, or productivity tools for follow-up and collaboration.

  • Adjustable Accuracy and Performance

Choose settings that fit your needs, whether you want faster processing, balanced performance, or higher transcription quality on supported devices.

Why Choose Priviscribe?

Priviscribe is more than a simple voice recorder. It combines offline speech recognition, speaker labeled transcripts, and privacy-first processing in one streamlined app. Many transcription apps rely on cloud uploads, but Priviscribe is designed for users who want more control over sensitive recordings and meeting content.

It is ideal for:

  • Business meetings and work conversations
  • Interviews and research recordings
  • Lecture notes and class discussions
  • Study sessions and revision
  • Personal voice memos and thought capture
  • Team collaboration and meeting follow-up
  • Private conversations that should remain on device

Privacy First

Your privacy matters. Priviscribe is built around on-device processing so your audio and transcripts do not need to leave your device for transcription. No sign-up. No unnecessary data collection. No cloud dependency for the core experience.

If you need an offline speech to text app for private meeting notes, interview transcription, lecture recording, speaker labeled transcripts, and secure voice to text workflows, Priviscribe is built for you.
