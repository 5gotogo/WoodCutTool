App Store description

What it does

Ritualix is a clean, privacy-first habit system that helps you build streaks without stress.

Plan tiny actions, run Pomodoro-style focus sessions, and keep your momentum with gentle reminders and Lock Screen widgets.

Why Ritualix

  • AI habit plans that turn big goals into bite-size steps
  • Streak Freeze to protect progress on tough days
  • Pomodoro focus timers for deep work and study
  • Lock Screen widgets for one-tap check-ins
  • Offline & private: no account required
  • Weekly review to reflect and adjust
  • Lightweight stats to see trends, not noise

How it works

1. Pick one small habit.

2. Start a focus session or log a quick check-in.

3. Keep your streak alive—Ritualix nudges you just enough to stay consistent.

Whether you’re building a morning routine, training for fitness, or studying with deep focus, Ritualix keeps you accountable with a minimalist flow that gets out of the way.
