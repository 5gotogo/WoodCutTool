What it does

SignatureMark is a creator-focused watermark and signature workspace designed to help you protect your work and build a consistent personal brand.

Unlike generic watermark or photo editing tools, SignatureMark centers around your identity. Create personalized text, handwritten, or image-based signatures, save them to your library, and apply them consistently across single photos or entire series.

Whether you are a photographer, designer, content creator, or social media publisher, SignatureMark helps you maintain a clean and recognizable signature style without complex editing.

Key Features:

  • Creator Profile & Brand Setup

Set your name, brand or studio, tagline, and default signature to personalize your workspace.

  • Signature Library

Create and manage multiple text, handwritten, or image signatures in one place.

  • Presets & Consistent Style

Save watermark presets and apply the same style across multiple photos for visual consistency.

  • Apply to Series

Batch apply your signature or watermark to multiple images with consistent positioning and appearance.

  • Flexible Customization

Adjust opacity, size, rotation, font, and placement to match your creative style.

  • Privacy-First & Offline

All processing is done locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded.

SignatureMark is not just a watermark maker — it is a signature system for creators who value originality and brand identity.
