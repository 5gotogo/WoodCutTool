What it does

SnapCleaner – Clean, Organize & Optimize Your iPhone Storage Effortlessly

SnapCleaner is the ultimate all-in-one storage management tool for iPhone, designed to free up space, optimize your device, and keep your photo, video, and file library perfectly organized. With smart AI detection, SnapCleaner automatically finds duplicates, similar items, and blurry photos or videos, making cleanup fast, safe, and reliable.

Powerful Features for Total Device Optimization:

1. Photo Cleaning and Organization – Detect duplicate, similar, and blurry photos in your library. Free up space while keeping your memories intact. SnapCleaner automatically sorts and optimizes your photo library for maximum efficiency.

2. Video Cleanup and Compression – Quickly find large or unnecessary videos. Compress videos to save storage without losing quality or delete unwanted files instantly. Efficiently manage video storage and always have space for new content.

3. File Scanning and Cleanup – Scan for large files, temporary files, and unused content cluttering your storage. Remove duplicates and unnecessary data to improve performance and reclaim space for important content.

4. Contact Organization – Merge duplicate contacts, remove blank or incomplete entries, and keep your address book clean and updated.

5. One-Tap Smart Scans – Start a full cleanup with a single tap. SnapCleaner’s AI scan automatically detects clutter across photos, videos, files, and contacts, saving time and effort.

6. AI Smart Scan Technology – Powered by AI, SnapCleaner identifies duplicate and similar content even when media looks slightly different. The AI selects items that matter least, allowing safe cleanup.

7. Multi-Language Support – SnapCleaner follows your system language automatically or can be set manually. Supported languages: English, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, and Arabic (Saudi Arabia).

Why SnapCleaner is Essential:

  • Maintain a clean, organized, and optimized device effortlessly.
  • Free up storage without losing important photos, videos, or files.
  • Automate cleanup with AI detection for duplicates, similar items, and blurry media.
  • Compress large videos, remove temporary files, and optimize storage in a few taps.
  • Manage contacts efficiently.
  • Multi-language support for global use.

Subscription Unlocks Full Power:

Subscribe to unlock unlimited cleaning, advanced AI duplicate and similar media detection, fast video compression, hidden metadata removal, and full access to all cleanup tools. Subscription includes priority support, frequent updates, and improved scanning accuracy. Some features are subscriber-only, enhancing your storage management experience.

Perfect for:

  • Travelers optimizing storage before a trip.
  • Users with limited iPhone space.
  • Professionals needing an organized device.
  • Anyone wanting automated cleanup for photos, videos, files, and contacts.

Subscription Benefits Include:

  • Unlimited access to all tools.
  • AI-powered duplicate and similar media detection.
  • Fast video compression.
  • EXIF and hidden metadata removal for privacy.
  • Priority support and frequent updates.

SnapCleaner ensures your iPhone is always organized, fast, and efficient. It removes clutter, optimizes storage, and helps maintain your device in excellent condition. No more manual searching for duplicates, large videos, or unused files—SnapCleaner handles it all.

Privacy & Security: All data is processed locally on your device. Your photos, videos, files, and contacts are never uploaded.
