What it does

SnapLabel is an AI-powered label maker that helps you create and print labels in seconds. Simply take a photo or choose one from your album, and SnapLabel will automatically recognize the content and suggest a clear label for you.

Whether you want to organize household items, office supplies, storage boxes, folders, products, or personal belongings, SnapLabel makes labeling faster, smarter, and more convenient.

Key Features:

  • AI Recognition: Detect objects, text, and categories from your photos to generate smart label suggestions
  • Photo and Album Import: Take a new picture or select an existing image from your library
  • Instant Label Maker: Create clear and useful labels with just one tap
  • Print Ready Output: Export labels for printing and use them on items, folders, shelves, boxes, or products
  • Smart Organization: Keep your home, office, and daily essentials neatly labeled and easy to manage
  • Simple and Efficient Workflow: Turn photos into practical labels without complicated steps

SnapLabel is designed for users who want a faster way to create labels for personal, home, school, or business use. With AI-powered recognition and an easy label-making experience, it helps reduce manual work and makes organization more efficient.

Subscription Notice:

A subscription is required to access the app’s features and create, export, or print labels. Please review the available subscription options in the app before purchasing.
