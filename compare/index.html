Woodworking Comparisons

Most woodworking decisions are really comparisons: which sheet material, which saw, which planning method. Each guide here puts two options side by side with a clear table, real trade-offs, and a recommendation by use case, so you can choose with confidence before you buy stock or make the first cut.

Plan the cut after you choose

Once you have picked a material and a saw, the next step is planning the cuts so you buy the right amount and waste less.
