Tinnitus Support

Support page for Tinnitus, an iPhone app for sound masking, frequency matching, hearing profile setup, and personal listening progress.

App Overview

Tinnitus helps users create a personal sound environment with masking sounds, adjustable noise layers, frequency matching tools, and a hearing profile workflow. It is designed as a self-guided wellness and listening aid app for iPhone.

CategoryHealth / Utilities
PlatformiOS (iPhone)
Version1.0.0
PrivacyOn-device settings and profiles

Main Features

  • Play masking and relief sounds such as white noise, pink noise, brown noise, rain, ocean, and wind.
  • Mix sounds and adjust playback volume for a more comfortable listening experience.
  • Use frequency matching to identify a reference tone separately for the left and right ear.
  • Save and enable a notch filter based on the selected reference frequency.
  • Create a hearing profile by listening through test tones and recording which frequencies are audible.
  • Review progress and adjust settings from the app's progress and settings screens.

Core User Flow

Step 1Choose a masking sound and keep the volume comfortable.
Step 2Use frequency matching to set the tone that most closely matches what you hear.
Step 3Save the result, enable the notch filter if desired, and revisit your hearing profile over time.

Important Health Notice

Tinnitus is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. Sound playback and frequency tools may not be appropriate for everyone. Stop using the app if you feel discomfort, keep volume low, and contact a qualified healthcare professional for medical questions, sudden hearing changes, pain, dizziness, or persistent symptoms.

Privacy and Data Handling

Tinnitus is designed around local app settings and on-device profiles. The app does not require an account for its core workflow. See the Tinnitus Privacy Policy.
