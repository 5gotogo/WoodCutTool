CutList Support

Support page for CutList, an iPhone utility app that turns cut lists into optimized sheet layouts fully offline.

App Overview

CutList is a utility and productivity app for woodworkers, cabinet makers, furniture builders, and DIY users who need to plan sheet-good cuts directly on iPhone.

CategoryUtilities / Productivity
PlatformiOS (iPhone)
Version1.0.0
PrivacyOffline project workflow

Main Features

  • Create cut lists from a mobile-first workflow.
  • Add part names, dimensions, quantities, and material notes.
  • Define sheet size, material, kerf, spacing, rotation, and grain direction settings.
  • Generate optimized sheet layouts on iPhone.
  • Export, print, or share results only when you choose an iOS system action.

Core User Flow

Step 1Create or open a project.
Step 2Add parts, sheet stock, kerf, and layout constraints.
Step 3Optimize the layout, inspect placement, then optionally export or print.

Privacy and Data Handling

Project data is processed offline on the user's device. CutList does not use a remote optimization server, public profiles, social feeds, or user-generated community content. See the CutList Privacy Policy.
