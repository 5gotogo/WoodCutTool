QuiltFit Privacy Policy

This policy explains how QuiltFit handles quilt projects, fabric details, cutting plans, and sewing progress on iPhone.

Effective Date: June 19, 2026 | Last Updated: June 19, 2026

QuiltFit is an iOS app for planning quilt layouts, fabric estimates, cutting plans, and sewing workflow. The app is designed around local project planning.

Information You Enter

You may enter quilt names, finished sizes, block dimensions, border settings, fabric names, backing and batting estimates, cut-piece lists, sewing notes, and project progress. This information is used only to manage quilt planning inside the app.

Local Storage

Saved quilt projects, fabric settings, cutting plans, and sewing progress are stored locally on your device. Removing the app may delete locally stored data unless it is included in Apple-managed backups.

Network and Accounts

QuiltFit does not require a customer account system for its core planning workflow. We do not operate a public profile, social feed, or cloud project publishing service for QuiltFit.

Exporting and Sharing

If you choose to export, print, or share a quilt plan, the selected content is passed to the iOS system option you invoke. The destination app, printer, or service may process that information according to its own policies.

What We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information. QuiltFit is not designed around advertising profiles, data brokerage, or third-party behavioral tracking.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete saved quilt projects and related planning data from within the app. You may also remove the app from your device to delete locally stored data, subject to Apple-managed backups.
