QuiltFit Support

Support page for QuiltFit, an iPhone utility app for quilt planning, fabric estimates, cutting plans, and sewing workflow.

App Overview

QuiltFit helps quilters plan finished quilt sizes, block layouts, fabric needs, cut-piece lists, and sewing progress from a mobile-first workflow.

CategoryUtilities / Lifestyle
PlatformiOS (iPhone)
Version1.0.0
WorkflowQuilt planning and cutting

Main Features

  • Calculate quilt block rows, columns, and finished top size.
  • Estimate backing, batting, binding, fabric width, and overage.
  • Build visual cutting plans and fabric shopping references.
  • Track cut pieces and sewing steps inside a project workflow.

Core User Flow

Step 1Create a quilt project and choose finished dimensions.
Step 2Add block size, seam allowance, borders, backing, and fabric setup.
Step 3Review estimates, cutting plan, and sewing progress.

Privacy and Data Handling

Quilt project data is intended to stay local to the user's device. QuiltFit does not center on social sharing, public profiles, or remote project publishing. See the QuiltFit Privacy Policy.
