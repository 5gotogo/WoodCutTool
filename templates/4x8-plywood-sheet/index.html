4x8 Plywood Sheet Template

Use this simple reference when sketching a plywood cutting layout by hand. A 4x8 sheet is commonly planned as 48 by 96 inches, but measure your actual material before cutting.

Planning Rules

Mark grain direction first, subtract any edge cleanup, include saw kerf between every part, and place the largest panels before shelves, dividers, cleats, or filler strips.

Template Reference

4x8 sheet: 48 in x 96 in
Kerf: use your blade width
Start: large panels
Then: repeated shelves or dividers
Finish: small fillers and reusable offcuts
