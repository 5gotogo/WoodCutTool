Important health notice

Tinnitus Relief is designed for relaxation, focus, and sound masking purposes only. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent tinnitus or any other medical condition. The Listening Profile and Frequency Match tools are comfort and personalization features, not clinical or audiological assessments. If you have persistent ringing, hearing changes, pain, dizziness, or other hearing concerns, consult a licensed healthcare professional or audiologist.
